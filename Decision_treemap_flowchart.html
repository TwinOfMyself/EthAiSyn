⚠ Gap 1 — No logging step
Neither IMH workflow records a verbal commitment made during a call. Node 1 adds it.
⚠ Gap 2 — No weekend handover
No protocol exists for open commitments crossing EOD or a weekend. Node 3 is the missing step.
⚠ Gap 3 — Broken promises invisible
Failed callbacks generate no signal. The Node 4 audit makes them countable and visible.
§ SAMHSA 2019
Follow-up within 24 hours is an institutional responsibility. Reflected in Node 2 and Node 5.
§ Joint Commission · SEA 58
Every handoff requires a documented action list and named owner. Reflected in Nodes 1, 2, 3.
§ OMH / DOH · Feb 2026
Failed attempts must be documented before disengagement. Reflected in Node 4 audit.

The current IMH workflow ends when the contact is made. This chart begins where that workflow ends — at the moment an obligation is created inside a completed call. Every node addresses a gap the current workflow structurally cannot see.
